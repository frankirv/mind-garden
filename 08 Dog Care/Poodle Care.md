# Poodle Care

## Food / Supplements
- Vitamins and collagen supplements for older dogs.
- Use only about 1/5 of the usual dose.
- Diet can include:
  - 1.5 blueberries daily
  - Small piece of crushed cabbage or lettuce

## Avoid
- Macadamia nuts
- Chocolate
- Grapes
- Acidic fruits like tomatoes
- Sunflower seed shells or dangerous small items

## Safety Notes
Dog likes to climb on tables, so keep harmful foods out of reach.

## Daily Checklist
- [ ] Food
- [ ] Water
- [ ] Supplements
- [ ] Check table/counter safety
- [ ] Walk / activity
