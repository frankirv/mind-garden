# Kitchen Appliance Setup

## Appliances
- Espresso machine
- Air fryer
- Rice cooker
- Water filter
- Electric steamer
- Water pot

## Main Concern
Small kitchen with limited counter space. Dining room has carpet, so water-related appliances need extra caution.

## Recommended Setup
- Keep water filter and espresso machine on a stable, waterproof surface.
- Use kitchen counter for high-heat or steam appliances when possible.
- Use a prep table for temporary cooking/prep, not permanent clutter.
- If using dining room, add a waterproof mat/tray under water appliances.

## Safety Checklist
- [ ] Heat-resistant mat for air fryer / steamer
- [ ] Waterproof tray under espresso / water filter
- [ ] Avoid carpet exposure to leaks
- [ ] Keep cords away from water
- [ ] Leave ventilation space around hot appliances
