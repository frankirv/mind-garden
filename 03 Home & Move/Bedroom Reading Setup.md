# Bedroom Reading Setup

## Goal
Create a quiet place for Bible reading, Kindle/iPad notes, or light study.

## Options
### Small Desk
Good if the goal is reading and note-taking only.

### Larger 55-inch Risable Table
Better if you want serious study space and already have enough room.

### Accent Chair + Side Table
Good for reading, but less ideal for writing or computer use.

## Recommendation
Use the bedroom for quiet reading, not a second full work office. A medium or larger desk is useful if the small table feels too limiting.

## Checklist
- [ ] Firm supportive chair
- [ ] Warm reading lamp
- [ ] Small shelf or Bible/book basket
- [ ] Minimal electronics
- [ ] Keep desk surface clear
