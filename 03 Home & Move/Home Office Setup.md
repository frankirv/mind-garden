# Home Office Setup

## Current Situation
- Living room often functions as office / study area.
- Two large monitors.
- Mac for development.
- Windows machine as local server / AI box.
- L-shaped desk and possible extra table.
- Separate guitar corner.
- Desire for focused study and Bible reading area.

## Principles
- Keep work/development zone separate from quiet reading zone.
- Avoid making desk layout too crowded.
- Use one strong main workstation instead of many small surfaces.
- Keep guitar accessible but visually organized.
- Use cable management and a small equipment shelf.

## Desk Layout Ideas
### Option A: Main L Desk + Separate Reading Table
Best for mental separation.

### Option B: U-shaped Desk
Best for equipment, but may feel crowded.

### Option C: Small Bedroom Reading Desk
Good for quiet reading if chair and lighting are comfortable.
