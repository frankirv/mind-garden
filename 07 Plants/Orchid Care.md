# Orchid Care

## Importance
Orchids are valuable and need careful care.

## Care Rules
- Water thoroughly.
- Do not leave standing water.
- Provide bright light but no direct sun.
- After flowers fall, trim stems slightly.
- Apply fertilizer once after trimming.
- Fertilize monthly during appropriate growth periods.

## Weekly Checklist
- [ ] Check roots
- [ ] Check potting medium moisture
- [ ] Water if needed
- [ ] Drain fully
- [ ] Check for direct sun exposure
