# Clivia Care

## Key Priorities
- Bright indirect light
- Avoid harsh direct sun
- Use well-draining soil
- Do not overpot too early
- Prefer pots with good height and drainage

## Potting Notes
- Height can matter more than width for root depth.
- Baby Clivia should not be disturbed too often.
- Terracotta helps drying but may dry too quickly outdoors.
- Plastic inner pot with many holes + decorative outer pot can work well.

## Patio Constraints
Apartment restrictions may limit umbrellas, privacy screens, or fixtures. Use movable shade from furniture or plant placement instead.
