# Plant Care Dashboard

## Plants
- Clivia
- Monstera
- Pothos / Snow Queen
- Chinese evergreen / Aglaonema
- Snake plant / Moonshine / Whale fin
- Philodendron Birkin
- Orchids
- Cacti

## Weekly Care
- [ ] Check soil moisture
- [ ] Water cacti if dry / weekly rhythm
- [ ] Water orchids thoroughly but avoid standing water
- [ ] Check light exposure
- [ ] Rotate plants if needed
- [ ] Inspect pests

## Monthly Care
- [ ] Fertilize indoor plants with organic fertilizer
- [ ] Use about one full plastic spoon per session
- [ ] Adjust watering based on season

## Important Notes
- Orchids: no direct sun; need bright light; no standing water.
- Cacti: need more sunlight.
- Clivia: protect from direct harsh sun; careful pot sizing.
