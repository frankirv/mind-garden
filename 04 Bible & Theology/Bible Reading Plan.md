# Bible Reading Plan

## Long-Term Goal
Read through the Bible many times with emphasis on:
- New Testament
- Psalms
- Proverbs

## Suggested Practical Plan
Instead of trying to track only total completions, track repeat exposure by section.

## Weekly Rhythm
- New Testament: daily
- Psalms: several times per week
- Proverbs: chapter-based rotation
- Old Testament: steady background reading

## Reflection Questions

- What does it reveal about Christ?

- What should I believe, repent of, or practice?
