# UPCI and Trinity Study

## Focus
Compare UPCI / Oneness Pentecostal interpretations with mainstream Christian doctrine, especially around the Trinity, baptism, and Christology.

## Key Questions
- Does Matthew 28:18–20 imply one personal name or the revealed authority of Father, Son, and Holy Spirit?
- How should John 1:1–5 be read?
- Is “the Word” merely God’s idea/mind, or personally distinct?
- How should Acts baptism passages relate to Matthew 28?
- What is the difference between emotional interpretation and textual interpretation?

## Study Method
1. Write the UPCI claim clearly.
2. List the biblical text.
3. Identify assumptions.
4. Compare with grammar, context, and historical Christian interpretation.
5. Write the strongest counterargument charitably.
